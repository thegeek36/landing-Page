Who We Are

Crafting Spaces with Soul

At Adwatika Interio & Decors, we believe every space is a canvas waiting to tell your story. Rooted in the rich cultural heritage of Odisha, we blend Pattachitra artistry with contemporary design to create interiors uniquely yours.

Our philosophy: give a personal touch to every work. We don't just design rooms — we craft experiences that resonate with who you are and how you live.

How We Work

Our 5-Step Design Process

From the first conversation to the grand reveal — a seamless, transparent journey crafted around you.

01

Discovery Call

We listen deeply to understand your vision, lifestyle, and aspirations.

02

Site Survey

Our team visits to measure and assess every inch of your space.

03

Design Concept

Bespoke concept with mood boards, 3D renders & material palette.

04

Execution

Skilled artisans bring the design to life with precision and craft.

05

Handover

Walk-through of your transformed space until every detail delights.
